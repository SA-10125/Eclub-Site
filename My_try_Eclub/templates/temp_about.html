Our Mission

E-CLUB is dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and innovation among students at PESU. We believe in the power of ideas and the potential of young minds to shape the future of business and technology.

What We Do

  • Organize workshops and seminars
  • Provide mentorship opportunities
  • Host startup competitions
  • Facilitate networking events

Our Values

  • Innovation and creativity
  • Collaboration and teamwork
  • Continuous learning
  • Ethical entrepreneurship

Our Journey

2018 - E-CLUB Founded

A group of passionate students came together to create a hub for entrepreneurship.

2019 - First Startup Weekend

Organized our inaugural 54-hour startup event, with over 100 participants.

2020 - Virtual Pivot

Successfully transitioned all events and mentorship programs to virtual platforms.

2021 - Launch of Incubator Program

Established a dedicated space and resources for student startups.

2022 - National Recognition

Received award for 'Best College Entrepreneurship Club' at a national conference.

2023 - Global Partnerships

Formed alliances with international entrepreneurship organizations and universities.
